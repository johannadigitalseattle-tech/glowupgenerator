✦ How to Get Viral AI Photos
Understanding how your choices affect the final image
🎨
What the Outfit Does in the Prompt
The outfit text gets inserted literally into the prompt. When you pick "Gold satin evening dress", the AI reads "She wears an elegant gold satin evening dress" and generates exactly that. The more specific the description, the better the image.
🔥
Fierce Goddess — Outfit Role
Here the outfit is a supporting detail. The stars are the lighting, hair, and skin. Any outfit works — but dark, barely-there, or dramatic pieces amplify the chiaroscuro effect.
💸
Money Boss — Outfit Role
The outfit feeds the abundance scene. It combines with the cash, neon signs and floating money. Power suits, blazers and bold colors work best here — they scream CEO energy.
💅
Beauty Editorial — Outfit Role
The outfit is secondary — the nails and hands are the stars. Choose something elegant but simple so it doesn't compete. Minimalist or satin works best.
🎀
Chic Editorial — Outfit is the Star
In this style the outfit IS the main subject. It gets described with more detail. High Fashion, Power Woman and Modern Chic categories work best here — they're designed for editorial fashion photography.
🎭
Concept / Themed — Match the Mono
The outfit should match the monochromatic background color. If your background is dark brown, choose a brown or chocolate outfit. The whole point is a seamless color story between skin tone, outfit and background.
✨
Why These 10 Categories Go Viral
Glam/Red Carpet → luxury editorial scenes · CEO/Power → authority and confidence · Summer/Vacation → bright colors and light · Minimalist → Pinterest aesthetic · Evening → cinematic lighting · Streetwear → urban attitude
💡
Pro Tip: Build a Color Story
The most viral photos have a unified color story: pick an outfit, then match the Dominant Color swatch and Background to it. Example: Champagne gown + Golden gradient background + Black Gold palette = editorial magazine shot.
